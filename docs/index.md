## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MoguMogu ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

MoguMogu is designed to work entirely on your device. We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Camera images or barcode scan results
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Account credentials (the App has no account system)

All barcode scanning and ingredient analysis is performed locally on your device using on-device machine learning. No scan data ever leaves your device.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

MoguMogu does **not** integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not use:

- Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics
- Facebook SDK
- Crashlytics or similar crash reporting tools
- Any advertising networks

## 4. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

- **Camera** — used solely to scan barcodes and food labels on your device. Images are processed locally and never uploaded.

No other permissions are requested.

## 5. Children's Privacy

MoguMogu is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info.hari07@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete it.

## 6. Data Security

Because MoguMogu does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data stored on our servers to secure. All processing happens on your device.
